About Me

A condensed timeline

  • 1992

    Born & Bred

    My hometown in North Carolina has more pigs than people. I grew up playing sports, crushing video games, and reading fiction. My high school mascot was the Dark Horses

  • 2010

    College Educated

    I graduated from The University of North Carolina with a major in Global Studies and a minor in Geography. While in college, I spent a semester at l'Université Mohammed V in Rabat, Morocco, where I studied French, Arabic, history, and politics

  • 2014

    International Experience

    After school, I taught English in France before moving to Goa, India. There I worked in stone exports before starting an import business back home. I then drove from NC to L.A., where I found a job promoting museum exhibits in Eastern Europe

  • 2016

    Professional Development

    I sharpened a variety of skills over the years in order to make the most of my personal and professional opportunities, and I benefitted tremendously from the wonderful people and places I met and saw along the way

  • 2018

    Continuing Education

    I've realized that I have an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and growth. I like to be challenged and learn new things. I intend to continue pushing myself and would love to work with others of a similar mindset

Donde

A social mapping and communication tool to be used during times of uncertainty

For our first group project, we created a mobile-responsive app for use as a means of communicating with your neighbors in times of crisis. Inspired by Hurricane Florence in 2018, the idea is that individuals use it to discover the latest news as well as real-time availability of resources.

  • Date: September 2018
  • Skills Used: Vue.JS, Firebase, Google Maps API
  • Team: Alexander Jones, Alfonso Montano, Giorgi Kornashvilli, Field Palmer
  • GitHub Repo
  • Deployed Site

Mobile Mechanic

An app connecting car owners with auto technicians at everyone's convenience

Providing people with a platform to get auto service where they want, when they want, and by whome they want. Providing talented mechanics with the opportunity to make some extra money and develop critical work experience. All of this takes place on one platform, where users can log in and easily connect with one another.

  • Date: November 2018
  • Skills Used: Passport, Materialize, Cheerio, MySQL, Stripe, Handlebars
  • Team: Alexander Jones, Alfonso Montano, Ethan Holston, Field Palmer

  • Deployed Site
