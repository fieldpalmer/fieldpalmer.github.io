About Me

Picardie, France, 2015

I'm from Clinton, North Carolina, a nice little town an hour and ten minutes southeast of Raleigh with more pigs than people. I graduated from UNC - Chapel Hill in 2014 with a BA and spent the next four years working in various parts of the world and traveling as much as I could. My favorite international locale to call home was Morocco. I've worked as an English teacher, a salesman, a marketer, a promoter, and a cook.

These experiences brought to the forefront my desire for <!-- *insert Liam Neeson voice* --> "a very particular set of skills." It was clear to me that future opportunities would increasingly be found in tech-related fields and the ability to build and communicate in the language of computers could open a lot of doors. I've always had a knack for picking up foreign languages, and I've found that learning programming is kinda just learning to speak the langauge of computers. My interests are broad and the specific direction is unclear, but I'm eager to obtain the skills necessary to productively pursue those interests personally and professionally.
